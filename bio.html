Bio

Stjepanek a multifaceted project initiated by the young Santiago Stjepanek from the roots of the Argentinian capital, Buenos Aires. From an early age he began having his attachment to electronic music, which led him to move to musical production, transforming different protocols and commands to layers of sound to generate different types of moments. This constant search to tell something led him to 4 years of learning in which he remains immersed to find the sound that identifies him most. A story through melancholy frequencies formed by emotional strings, constant arpeggios, and percussion, invites us to be part of this... a story made of dreams. His music caught the attention of Fideles, Hernan Cattaneo, SOEL.
